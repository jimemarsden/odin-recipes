Orecchiette

Orecchiette is an italian pasta named for the shape it takes. In Italian, Orecchiette means "ear".

There are many ways to make this, and many different ingredients to use, but this is the way I make it.

Ingredients for Main Part

  • Orecchiette Pasta (or medium shells)
  • Spinach
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Beef stock
  • large white onion
  • garlic

Ingredients for seasoning

  • Brown sugar
  • onion powder
  • garlic powder
  • oregano
  • basil
  • salt
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Brown beef with seasoning and chopped onion in pot
  2. Pour in beef broth
  3. Allow broth to reduce halfway
  4. pour in 1lb of pasta and let cook, stirring frequently
  5. Once pasta is full cooked and the broth sauce is goopy, fold in spinach
  6. turn off heat
  7. allow to cool, and serve with parmesan cheese
